MANUAL

Mad Bomber tries to kill you with loads of bombs. Will you let him do that...? Catch all the bombs to deactivate them!

CONTROLS

Press ← to move left or → to move right.

DIFFICULTY & SCORE

There are 10 levels of difficulty - Mad Bomber speeds up! You get squared level number amount of points for every caught bomb. After reaching 10th level, each bomb is worth 100 points.
